# Project Description

##Theme
A risky decision-making simulator where the player tries to make money while avoiding legal trouble.

##Goal
Make as much money as possible before time runs out without getting caught or locked up.

##Planned classes
plan to create classes such as Player, Action, Authority, and Game. The Player class will store the player's money and suspicion level. The Action class will represent different choices the player can make. The Authority class will handle encounters with law enforcement. The Game class will control the overall gameplay.

##Gameplay Each turn, the player chooses between risky money-making actions, safer legal actions, talking to authorities, lying low, or spending money to reduce suspicion.

##Limited resources Time, money, suspicion level, and trust with authorities.

##Plan for handling time
The game will be divided into days. Each action uses one day, and the player has a limited number of days to earn as much money as possible before the game ends.

##Mapping
The game will use a text-based map where the player can travel between different locations such as neighborhoods, businesses, hideouts, and the police station.

##Tradeoff system
Risky choices earn more money but increase suspicion. Safer choices earn less but keep you out of trouble. Talking to authorities can lower suspicion, but it may cost money, time, or trust.

##Win condition
Finish with the most money while staying under the suspicion limit.

##Lose condition
Suspicion gets too high and the player gets arrested/locked up, or time runs out with too little money.
